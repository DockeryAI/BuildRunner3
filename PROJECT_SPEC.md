## User Stories

- As a trip organizer, I want to create trail runs with assigned leaders and participants, so that I can coordinate multiple groups efficiently throughout the day
- As a participant, I want to view my personalized checklist and assigned tasks, so that I know exactly what to bring and what responsibilities I have
- As a meal coordinator, I want to assign people to specific meals and see what they're cooking, so that we can ensure proper meal planning for the group
- As a trail leader, I want to select and sequence obstacles with difficulty ratings, so that I can plan appropriate routes for my assigned participants
- As any user, I want to view the daily agenda and drill into specific activities, so that I know the schedule and details for each part of the trip
